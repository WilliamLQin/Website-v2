Robotics


I am a registered participant of FIRST® Robotics Competition!

Currently, I am a programmer of FRC Team 4308.

As a programmer, I program the autonomous and teleoperated driver controls for the robot, as well as create applications to serve the team. I also am a member of the team, and we compete against other robots in a special "sport" that changes theme every year!


The 2017 season game: Steamworks!


In the past, I was a programmer of FRC Team 1325 for the 2016 and 2017 seasons. During my time on this team, I led the team scouting initiative and deployed a special compiler that would visualize the data collected by the team!

The scouting compiler was a web application and database for recording and viewing information on other teams at FIRST Steamworks competitions. It was extensively used for important insights on other teams prior to competitions and for alliance selections.

The project was built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Firebase.
